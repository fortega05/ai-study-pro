# AI Study Pro 🚀

AI Study Pro is a modern, lightweight web application designed to help students and lifelong learners master their subjects through interactive flashcards. It features a sleek, responsive interface with a focus on ease of use and visual clarity.

## 🌟 Features

* **Interactive Flashcard Creation**: Easily build custom study sets with a fluid, row-based interface [cite: uploaded:createSet.html, uploaded:createLogic.js].
* **Smart Library Management**: Organize your learning with a dedicated dashboard featuring smooth pagination (10 sets per page) to keep your workspace clean [cite: uploaded:viewSets.html, uploaded:viewLogic.js].
* **Sleek Pagination**: A minimalist, "pill-style" navigation bar with a "Jump to Page" feature for quick access to large libraries [cite: uploaded:viewLogic.js].
* **Dark & Light Mode**: Seamless theme switching that persists across sessions, preventing visual flashes during page loads [cite: uploaded:theme.js, uploaded:header.html].
* **Performance Optimized**: Utilizes asynchronous component fetching (Header/Footer) and CSS layout preservation to eliminate layout shifts and flickering [cite: uploaded:style.css, uploaded:viewSets.html].
* **Local Storage Persistence**: Your study sets and theme preferences are saved directly to your browser—no account required for local use [cite: uploaded:createLogic.js, uploaded:theme.js].

## 🔮 Future Roadmap (AI Integration)

The ultimate goal for **AI Study Pro** is to revolutionize how you prepare for exams:
* **Auto-Flashcard Generation**: Simply copy and paste your lecture notes or textbook chapters, and the AI will automatically extract key terms and definitions to create sets for you.
* **Automated Testing**: The AI will generate practice tests and quizzes based on your notes to identify gaps in your knowledge.
* **Smart Summaries**: Turn long-form content into bite-sized, digestible study materials instantly.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

* **Frontend**: HTML5, CSS3 (Inter font family), Vanilla JavaScript [cite: uploaded:style.css, uploaded:index.html].
* **State Management**: Browser LocalStorage [cite: uploaded:createLogic.js].
* **Design**: Modern, card-based UI with responsive layouts [cite: uploaded:style.css].
